FAQ

Common questions.

Do you deliver and set up the RV?

Yes, delivery and setup may be available depending on distance, dates, and campground rules.

Are pets allowed?

Pets may be allowed with approval and an additional pet fee. Please include pet details in your booking request.

Is smoking allowed?

No. Smoking, vaping, and strong odors are not allowed inside the RV.

Do I need RV experience?

For delivered rentals, RV experience is usually not needed. We will explain the basics during setup.

Is a security deposit required?

Yes. A refundable security deposit may be required to cover damage, missing items, or excessive cleaning.
